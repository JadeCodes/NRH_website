"Balayage is the go-to for modern, chic hair, creating depth & dimension, & leaving you with that sun kissed finish."

Balayage is a french colour technique that was developed in the 1970's.

Its a free hand technique that is applied by hand rather than the traditional foils & can be used on the shortest pixie crop to the longest mane.

Balayage is more fashionable & cost effective than foils. It's what all the A-lister celebrities are wearing (think Gisele Bundchen, Heidi Conley, Jessica Biel), because it gives such a natural, beautiful, healthy finish with no stripey lines or block colours.

Price List

Balayage is more forgiving as it doesn't leave a demarcation line making your visits to the salon less frequent & better for the hair's condition.
