Natasha Radonich

With over 20 years experience in hairdressing I still love my Job. I absolutely love making people feel amazing about themselves.

I grew up & started my career in Christchurch, then off to London. Now based in Auckland it was time for me to open a salon that is all about the client. Warm, welcoming & private.

I wanted to be tucked away from the hussel & bussel & create a space clients can feel at ease. I love coming to work!

Hollie Gray

Hollie is Natasha's Senior Colour Technician.

Hollie started hairdressing at the age of 15 & has freelanced for over 10 years, in New Zealand & London, specializing in colour where her true passion and eye is. Hollie is very arty & expressive & loves creating natural, beautiful colour work keeping the hair in its best condition possible.

Having worked in many large salons, being in a small artistic space with just one other talented hairdresser Natasha, is her ultimate.
